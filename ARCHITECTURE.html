6-FACTOR SCORING ENGINE

bounty-manager.js · Each CVE x program scored independently
High Weight

Tech Stack Match — 30 pts

CVE description/CPE keywords vs program techStack array. Most important factor.

CWE Relevance — 20 pts

Is the CVE's CWE in the program's high-value list? (e.g., CWE-79, CWE-89, CWE-918)

CVSS Score — 15 pts

Normalized: cvss / 10 x 15. Higher severity = higher priority.

Signal Factors

Exploit Available — 15 pts

Has PoC code or listed in CISA KEV? Exploit availability = higher payout probability.

Freshness — 10 pts

<24h = 10 pts, <72h = 7, <7d = 4, else 0. Speed is money in bug bounties.

Competition Level — 10 pts

Independent programs = 10 (fewer reporters). HackerOne/Bugcrowd = 5 (crowded).
